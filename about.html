About Us

We're on a mission to revolutionize how students discover and secure internships that align with their skills, interests, and career aspirations.

Our Story

InternMatch was born from a simple observation: finding the right internship shouldn't be like searching for a needle in a haystack. Students deserve a smarter, more personalized approach to discovering opportunities that truly match their potential.

Founded in 2024, we've helped thousands of students connect with internships that kickstart their careers. Our AI-powered platform analyzes your unique profile, preferences, and goals to deliver recommendations that matter.

Today, we're proud to partner with leading companies across industries, creating a thriving ecosystem where talent meets opportunity.
